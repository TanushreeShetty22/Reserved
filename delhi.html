    Here are some of the best, Fine Dine restaurants in Delhi


    Bukhara - ITC Maurya

    A legendary, 4 star rated North Indian outlet in the city serving delectable dishes. They have got one of the most vintage outlooks with their dim lights and brown furniture. One is sure to have a soothingtime here over luxurious drinks and live entertainment.

    Kwality, Restaurant & Bar

    With its casual and relaxed vibe, an extensive menu of both authentic and reimagined dishes and heady cocktails, Boteco- Restaurante Brasileiro captures the essence of a Brazilian watering hole with an emphasis on delicious, high-quality food and great drinks.

    Ladera

    The restaurant is all dressed up in a rustic European style with a theme of blue and white. Apart from the shades of blue and white, the flowing fabric weaving through the entire restaurant attracts you towards its warm ambience. The vibe is striking as the very first glance at the Italian restaurant will suddenly transport you to Santorini because of the finished Greek sculptures at the restaurant.




    Rs.2000 - 2500 for one
